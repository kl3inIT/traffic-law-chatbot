- **Files modified:** `ChatClientConfig.java`

- **Files modified:** Both integration test files

- **Fix:** Removed Spring context; built services directly using `ChatClient.builder(chatModel).build()` and a mocked `AiParameterSetRepository` â†’ `ActiveParameterSetProvider` â†’ policy constructors
- **Files modified:** `ChatFlowIntegrationTest.java`
- **Commit:** c62478d

- **Files modified:** `AppPropertiesTest.java`
