| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-02-01 | Tampering | ChatQuestionRequest | mitigate | Constrain input with `@NotBlank` and `@Size(max = 4000)` and route validation failures through existing `ProblemDetail` handling. |

| T-02-03 | Information Disclosure | AnswerComposer | mitigate | `REFUSED` and `LIMITED_GROUNDING` outputs suppress unsupported legal sections per D-05/D-06 instead of leaking model-only advice. |
| T-02-04 | Repudiation | Response contract drift | mitigate | Serialization and composer tests pin exact field names and critical Vietnamese disclaimer/refusal strings so contract changes are observable in CI. |
</threat_model>
